# CPU Club Portfolio

> President of CPU Club | ISIMM | Academic Year 2024–2025

---

# Overview

This repository highlights my experience as President of the CPU Club (Club de Programmation et Univers Informatique) at the Higher Institute of Computer Science and Mathematics of Monastir (ISIMM) during the 2024–2025 academic year.

As President, I worked alongside an outstanding executive committee and dedicated members to organize engineering competitions, technical workshops, university events, and collaborative projects that promoted innovation, robotics, embedded systems, and programming.

Our objective was to create an active learning environment where students could strengthen their technical skills while developing teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving abilities.

---

# My Responsibilities

As President, I was responsible for:

- Leading a club of more than 70 active members.
- Coordinating the executive committee and technical teams.
- Planning the club's annual activities.
- Organizing robotics and programming competitions.
- Coordinating technical workshops for university students.
- Contacting companies and industry partners for collaboration.
- Supporting different committees throughout event preparation.
- Supervising logistics before and during major events.
- Promoting teamwork and student engagement.

These achievements were made possible through the collective efforts of the executive committee, committee leaders, volunteers, and all club members.
